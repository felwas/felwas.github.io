Most Influential Saudi Artists

Manal

Manal AlDowayan's work encompasses black and white photography, sculpture, video, sound, neon and large-scale participatory installation.

Shaweesh

Shaweesh is a young Saudi street artist based in Riyadh, part of a growing movement of young street artists in the country. He believes in initiating dialogue on the streets with his interpretations and impressionable art.

Moath

CBased in Almadina Almonawara, Saudi Arabia. Alofi is a visual and interactive artist that basis his installations and projects on Almadina's heritage and history.

Ajlan

Abdullnasser Gharem's brother and co-founder of Studio Gharem in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. Ajlan made controvortial works of art that are currently on a tour in the United States showcasing contemporary art in Saudi Arabia.
